The Process – What to Expect!

So you’re starting a new renovation! Congratulations! Here is what you should expect from Cutting Edge Surfaces to help make this experience as painless as possible. We will guide you through each step of the process, from selecting the perfect natural stone for your project to understanding the fabrication process, installation and maintenance. To receive a brochure with future details of this process, Please contact us and we will be happy to provide you with one.

ESTIMATE:

Call or Email us to schedule your free estimate today. One of our representatives will promptly schedule your free estimate. Our representatives will have granite samples, different edge option guide, and different sinks options for your project. We know each project is unique and encourage you to ask our representatives any questions you may have during this time.

Template:

On the day of the template you can expect our team to be there anywhere between 1 to 2 hours depending on your kitchen size. In order to template we ask that you have all necessary appliances and cabinets installed. You do not need to have all items off the current countertops. After this day we ask that any adjustments you may want to make to your kitchen countertops be advised to our team as these are the exact dimensions we will fabricate your kitchen. If you are providing the sink we will take it with us to our shop to make the necessary cuts. On this we will schedule a day to hand select your slabs, finalize details (sink type, edging, etc.) and sign agreement for your project

Material Selection:

You will hand select your slab(s) for your project at our office or any one of our local reputable stone suppliers. We encourage that on this day you bring along any cabinet doors, tile or paint colors would like to match for your project. Once at our supplier advise them that your fabricator is Cutting Edge Surfaces this will ensure you that the hand selected slabs you chose will be properly placed on hold. From this day we have a 1 to 3 day lead time.

Options:

Our sales staff will detail various edge options, surface finishes, cut-outs, and give you an explanation of the differences between types of natural stone and product extras.

Finalizing the Project:

After the materials have been selected and the project detailed a contract will be created and reviewed with you by our sales staff. The contact will have all the finalized specifics for the project detailed (e.g edge detail, cut-outs, custom finishes, etc)

Deposits:

Once a contract is signed, a 50% deposit of the final bill is required. The remaining balance is due upon delivery/installation.

Installation:

Installation occurs approximately 5-7 working days from the day of template. On the day of the installation you can expect our team to arrive promptly at the time previously scheduled with you. . Please help us in securing a clear walking path from the back or front door and into work area (kitchen, bathroom, etc.). Please remove any items from your countertops. Our normal installation requires only 1 day. Our team will leave everything in the exact manner as in which we arrived. Once complete we seal your countertops.

Final Walk-Through:

Finally, we will perform the FINAL WALK THROUGH to ensure the finished job meets our standard of quality and that you, the client, are totally satisfied with the work and service performed.

Maintenance:

Ongoing stone care maintenance instructions will be discussed and given to clients upon installation.

Warranty:

Cutting Edge Surfaces offers a 5 year warranty on the installation of countertops

with your new renovation, and let us know if we can help in any way for your future projects.
