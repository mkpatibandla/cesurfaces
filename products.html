Products

Cutting Edge Surfaces is the leader in fabrication and installation of natural stone countertops. With great experience in the industry, we can proudly say we have made a science of fabricating natural stone surfaces. Find out more about some of the natural stone products that we work with:

Granite

Natural Strength and Durability

Granite comes in a wide range of colors and styles, and the durability, longevity, and economy of granite make it a great material for heavily-used areas of your home, like countertops, table tops, and floors.

Read more about Granite >>

 

Marble

Beauty and Elegance

Marble is known for its beauty and elegance, and is a popular material for surfaces like countertops, floors, fireplace facings, as well as hearths, walls, and windowsills. Marble is well suited for areas that are frequently wet – allowing for a wide range of applications, including tub decks and showers.

Read more about Marble >>

 

Quartzite

Strong, durable, and resistant to abrasions

Quartzite is a metamorphic rock that can be exceptionally strong, hard, and dense. Because it is strong, durable, and resistant to abrasions, it can be used in a wide range of applications, including heavy-use surfaces like flooring, stairs, and countertops.

Read more about Quartzite >>

Soapstone

Soapstone is highly heat-resistant.

Soapstone, unlike some other natural stones, is not particularly abrasion or scratch resistant, but is often used as a flooring and countertop surface. It is highly heat-resistant, making it popular for use in fireplace surrounds.

Read more about Soapstone >>

 

 

Silestone

Silestone combines the benefits of quartz with antibacterial protection.

Used frequently in kitchens and bathrooms, Silestone combines the benefits of quartz (including its strength and durability!) with antibacterial protection. Silestone is non-porous, and is resistant to stains from everyday substances.
