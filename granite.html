Granite

Granite is a natural stone, and has been used for centuries as a building material. As an igneous rock (formed by volcanic activity), it is known for its strength and durability.

Natural Strength and Durability

Granite comes in a wide range of colors and styles, and the durability, longevity, and economy of granite make it a great material for heavily-used areas of your home, like kitchen countertops, table tops, and floors. Unlike some synthetic surfaces (where scratches are easily made), granite is usually harder than most utensils that would be used on them, making granite a strong and durable material that doesn’t show much wear.

Granite is an excellent choice for kitchen countertops, floors, and other surfaces that see heavy use.

Benefits of Granite:

  • Strong
  • Resistant to Abrasions
  • Very durable, even outdoors
  • Affordable
  • Mostly Stain Resistant

 

Drawbacks of Granite:

  • Direct application of heat is discouraged, as this can sometimes initiate cracking
