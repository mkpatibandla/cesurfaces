Marble

Marble is a metamorphic rock, and can be found in the mountains of most countries around the world. Marble is known for its beauty and elegance, and is a popular material for surfaces like countertops, floors, fireplace facings, as well as hearths, walls, and windowsills. Marble is well suited for areas that are frequently wet – allowing for a wide range of applications, including tub decks and showers.

Beauty and Elegance

Marble is known for its beauty and elegance, and is well-suited for areas that are frequently wet, allowing for a wide range of applications.

Benefits of Marble:

  • Beautiful
  • Elegant
  • Works well for wet surfaces – like showers

 

Drawbacks of Marble:

  • Marble is vulnerable to mild acids, like those that are frequently found in kitchens.
  • Exterior marble can be affected by acid rain and other weather weathering elements
