Quartzite

Formed from sandstone, Quartzite is a metamorphic rock that can be exceptionally strong, hard, and dense. Because it is strong, durable, and resistant to abrasions, it can be used in a wide range of applications, including heavy-use surfaces like flooring, stairs, and countertops. Quartzite can look like marble, while having many of the properties of granite. Quartzite comes in a range of shades, making it an excellent choice for a variety of styles and decor preferences.
